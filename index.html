About Me

I am a Pittsburgh-based software engineer and an incoming MSML student at Carnegie Mellon University.

Bio Sketch

Originally from Chicago, I graduated from the University of Pennsylvania in 2022 after completing software engineering internships at Facebook and the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative. I joined Facebook/Meta after graduation as a software engineer in the Reality Labs NYC division, working on brain-computer interfaces and on-device machine learning. I relocated to Pittsburgh and have been with Daedalus, Inc. since 2024.

I currently work on embedded software, machine learning, and app development at Daedalus, a boutique product design and engineering firm in Pittsburgh. We partner with local and international companies to develop products in the medical, safety, and manufacturing industries. Check out daed.com to learn more.
